  • What not to do
    During these hard times our live have been shifted in a way it has never been before. It brought such a sudden change and we werent ready for it. Seniors like myself were robbed of our last months with the people we spent our whole highschool years with. Others lost their jobs and lost relatives. These times are hard, as New yorkers we aren't used to staying in one place. We are used to a fast pace, going to school, going to practice or going to the city. People have been worrying and stressing themselves out. But dont FREAK OUT. take this time to do something new. Maybe start working out, try learning a new language. keep your mind distracted. Take care of your self and check on your friends. Check on your elderly neighbors maybe they will need some help. Help us all out and stay inside, Im pretty sure you have seen how people arent social distancing. These are the people who are keeping us from slowing this pandemic. Be wise and stay safe :)
